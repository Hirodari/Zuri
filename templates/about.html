About Zurri Investment CC

Zurri Investment CC is a company registered under the Namibian Legislation, owned by Nyota Bitenyo Karume and Justin Karume. We are a company dedicated to the food industry, with our current focus being Chilli sauce manufacture and retail.

We strive to provide organic chilli sauce, becoming an industry leader by providing memorable flavours of Africa through our product. We are based in the Klein Windhoek, Windhoek and can supply retailers in surrounding areas such as Rehoboth and Okahandja.
