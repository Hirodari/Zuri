About Us

About Zurri Investment CC

Zurri Investment CC is a company registered under the Namibian Legislation, owned by Nyota Bitenyo Karume and Justin Karume. We are a company dedicated to the food industry, with our current focus being Chilli sauce manufacture and retail.

We strive to provide organic chilli sauce, becoming an industry leader by providing memorable flavours of Africa through our product. We are based in the Klein Windhoek, Windhoek and can supply retailers in surrounding areas such as Rehoboth and Okahandja.

Why Choose Us

Our Products

We specialize in chilli sauces, currently producing two flavours of chillies Mild and Hot, both of which have been welcomed by the public for their unique taste and presentation. The ingredients involved in the creation of our sauce are meticulously selected ensuring high quality organic chilli sauce is produced. Zuri Pilipili is 100% organic. It contains no artificial preservatives as the product’s freshness is conserved through natural elements.

Phase 1

The chillies are freshly hand picked and 100% organic and plant-based.

Phase 2

Chillies are ground into soft paste, other ingredients are also added.

Phase 3

Chillies are boiled in a pot for a certain period of time.

Phase 4

Chilli sauce is then packed into sterilized bottles.

Phase 5

The bottles are firmly sealed, labelled and ready to be packed into boxes.
